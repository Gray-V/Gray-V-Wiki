Overview

Villains is a tabletop role-playing game (TTRPG) that emphasizes storytelling and the thrill of villainy. Designed for imaginative play, it allows players to embody super-powered antagonists, engaging in acts of nefarious creativity alongside friends.

Key Features

  • Role-play as diverse villain archetypes such as Assassins, Fanatics, Mutants, and Psychopaths.
  • Comprehensive Game Master (GM) Handbook to guide adventure creation and narrative flow.
  • Action cards and rule documents to facilitate smooth gameplay.
  • Encourages collaborative storytelling and imaginative scenario building.
