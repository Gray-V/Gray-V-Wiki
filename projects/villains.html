Overview

Villains is a tabletop role-playing game (TTRPG) that blends the excitement of TTRPGs with the structure of card games. Players take on the role of super villains navigating the city of Exilon to complete objectives, battle heroes, and indulge in acts of villainy. The game emphasizes collaborative storytelling, improvisation, and imaginative gameplay.

Playtesting and Prototyping

The development process involved extensive playtesting and paper prototyping to refine the game mechanics and balance gameplay. Early prototypes utilized paper components for cards, boards, and rule references, which allowed for rapid iteration and feedback collection.

Key Playtesting Outcomes:

  • Simplified character creation and gameplay flow to reduce barriers for new players.
  • Introduced event cards and action cards to streamline GM improvisation and maintain thematic consistency.
  • Removed complex currency mechanics to focus on core gameplay and player interaction.
  • Iteratively adjusted skills and powers to enhance balance and alignment with design goals.

Key Features

  • Play as diverse villain archetypes such as Assassins, Psychopaths, and Fanatics.
  • Streamlined Game Master (GM) tools including pre-built event cards and location decks.
  • Action cards to facilitate player autonomy and emphasize villainous creativity.
  • Collaborative gameplay with a focus on improvisation and team dynamics.
